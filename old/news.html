  New Updates:

July 1st, 2007
New design! So I have overhauled my site and working the kinks out of the new design. My lack of udpating and exhibiting my work lately has been due to the fact that my husband and I bought our first home in April. It took two months of renovating but we moved in June 1st and are settling in quite nicely. I now reside in beautiful Mimico village on the border of Toronto and Etobicoke. I am currently working on setting up my own home studio so I can get back to the business of making art.
